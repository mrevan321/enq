Our Food

Welcome to Eastern Noodle 'Que

We serve Mongolian style noodle dishes Ramen bowls and Eastern inspired pizzas. Our slogan is fresh Fusion made custom.
It can be hard to find a good Mongolian BBQ spot in Northern California much less one that uses freshly prepared ingredients. We aim to change that. We smoke meat cook and Grill everything on site in a vegetarian friendly environment.
Noodles and ramen not your thing? Try kimchi on a pizza or in a bowl of fried rice. We can whip you up a rice bowl or cook you up a whole batch for your next party or get-together. Have us over for pizza and we can do slices or whole 12" pies with whichever toppings you want from our selection.

MENU

Our drop-off catering menu is perfect for any occasion and comes fully equipped with plates utensils, napkins, and tongs. Each tray feeds 10-12 people.

Full Service Catering

Requires a $100 deposit in advance $12 per person per hour On-site catering for a minimum of 20 people for events of 2 hours minimum Each guest chooses from noodles or rice as a base Unlimited Ramen or rice bowls for each person Cooked and prepared onsite with 2 flat top grills and a wok We provide napkins, plastic ware, and plates a Mongolian style set up of fresh veggies, meats, and variety of sauces for each guest to enjoy



Pizza Catering

Requires a $100 deposit in advance $8 per person per hour with a minimum of 30 guest From Eastern Inspired Pizza recipes to your Classic pizza recipes we can do both unlimited for a minimum of 2 hours. We provide napkins, plates, and pizza spatulas. Call to discuss what toppings you would like for your next event.

Catering

Live catering is available for groups of 30 or greater, just give us a safe spot outside to cook for you.

We can do drop-off catering for businesses, private events or parties smaller than 30 people.

Drop off catering will be a set number of dishes you select. The number would also depend on if you wanted individual servings, or whether you would prefer a family-style buffet setup. We occasionally may be available an hour or two before drop off time to prepare the dishes on site.

Live catering can be served similarly to drop off with the option of individual dishes made to order, or with large servings of each dish to share.

Allow THREE days notice when scheduling drop-off catering
Allow TWO to THREE WEEKS notice for live catering
